# Big Lots (big-lots)
Big Lots is a discount retailer offering a wide assortment of brand-name closeout merchandise including furniture, food, consumables, seasonal products, soft home, hard home, and electronics. Big Lots operated hundreds of stores across the United States. In 2024, Big Lots filed for bankruptcy and its brand and operations were acquired by Nexus Capital Management.
